About Us

Phytochemical Explorer is a curated database aimed at supporting natural products researchers, biochemists, and pharmaceutical scientists by providing structured information on phytochemicals, their sources, and geographical origins. This project was developed with the goal of enabling easy access to large-scale compound data extracted from scientific literature.

Whether you're conducting machine learning on bioactive compounds or simply exploring ethnobotanical knowledge, our tool helps bridge the gap between complex data and meaningful discovery.
